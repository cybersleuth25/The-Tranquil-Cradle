The Retreat

Built by hand,
embraced by forest.

This isn't a typical resort. It’s a vision realized over years of careful planning, designed to preserve the wild integrity of the Western Ghats. We built The Tranquil Cradle with deep respect for the land, using minimal footprint structures and local laterite earth.

Surrounded by ancient trees and active coffee plantations, the property offers a rare kind of isolation. Here, comfort is defined by absolute quiet, misty mornings, and an unfiltered connection to an ecosystem much older than us.
